🔧 COOKIE FIX - RESTART BACKEND

PROBLEM: Cookie not being sent with /auth/me request

SOLUTION: Changed cookie settings from samesite="lax" to samesite="none"

WHY: Frontend (localhost:3000) and Backend (127.0.0.1:8000) are on different ports
     samesite="lax" doesn't allow cross-port cookies
     samesite="none" allows cross-port cookies

WHAT TO DO:

1. STOP BACKEND
   In terminal running backend
   Press: Ctrl+C

2. RESTART BACKEND
   cd backend
   uvicorn app.main:app --reload --host 127.0.0.1 --port 8000

3. TEST LOGIN
   - Open http://localhost:3000/auth/login
   - Email: bryantech.dev1@gmail.com

   - Click Login

4. VERIFY SUCCESS
   ✅ Redirected to dashboard

   ✅ Can see "My Posts" section

THAT'S IT! 🎉

The cookie will now be sent with every request.
